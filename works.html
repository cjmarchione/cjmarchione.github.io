About John Herbert Company


John Herbert Company was founded by Herbert Sandler and business partner “John” in 1954. After a short lived partnership selling office equipment, typewriters, etc., company transitioned into office furniture/design and was the Hudson Valley’s leading Steelcase dealer for many years. In 1987, following the financial markets crash, Steelcase & Mr. Sandler parted ways, opening the door for a more diversified product offering. This was the beginning of the company’s renewed future, as it grew and gained momentum in the commercial flooring market. Today, John Herbert Company sells all types of soft and hard surface flooring products, primarily to commercial/industrial clients. In addition, the company owns and operates concrete resurfacing/leveling equipment, to insure that installation prep requirements are strictly met, on time and within budget.
